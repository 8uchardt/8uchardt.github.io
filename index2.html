LIMBO


  • Game type: 2D Puzzle Platformer
  • Period: January 2008 - December 2010
  • Status: Out on XBoxOne, PS4, Steam, X360, PS3, PSVita, Steam, iOS
  • Developer: Playdead


My Role

As a member of the development team for the indie game Limbo, I played an integral role in creating the game's challenging and immersive gameplay experience. Specifically, I focused on:

  • Creating and refining the game's intricate puzzles to ensure that they were both challenging and rewarding
  • Working closely with the lead designer to iterate on and perfect the game's puzzles, utilizing my expertise to ensure each puzzle was challenging, yet fair and engaging
  • Heading up the game's quality assurance efforts, including bug testing and ensuring that the game was fully optimized for a smooth and seamless gameplay experience

My attention to detail and commitment to quality helped to ensure that Limbo was a well-designed and challenging game that resonated with players and critics alike. My contributions to the game's mechanics were instrumental in establishing Limbo's reputation as a classic in the indie game genre.
