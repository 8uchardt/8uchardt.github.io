About

I have 15+ years of experience in game design and digital-physical play development. This includes work on LIMBO and INSIDE at Playdead, For Honor at Studio Gobo, and seven years at the LEGO Group with LEGO Super Mario and most recently I worked with Play Software's experimentation projects.

My background is in systems design, level design, scripting and playtesting. I also hold a Cand. Mag. in Humanistic Informatics with a focus on Game Design from Aalborg University (2007).

  • Experience: 15+ years
  • Key projects: LEGO Super Mario (12 SKU waves), LIMBO, INSIDE, For Honor
  • Current work: Paid Garden Leave
  • Education: Cand. Mag. Humanistic Informatics, Game Design, Aalborg University
  • Skills: C#, YAML, Unity 3D, Unreal Engine, rapid prototyping, playtesting

Approach

I work with rapid prototyping and playtesting, often with players and end-users, to understand what feels natural and enjoyable. This has been part of my work at Playdead, Studio Gobo and the LEGO Group.
